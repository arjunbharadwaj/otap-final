Frequently Asked Questions

Is OTAP another wallet service provider?

No. Rather OTAP is platform where you can pay your local grocery stores, restaurants, salons, etc. using any of the cards/netbanking or wallet solutions like Paytm (others to follow soon)

Is my data safe?

All our payment partners are PCI-DSS complaint. In other words, do not worry. All your card data is stored in secure servers that meet the best of industry security standards

Is it going to cost me?

For most merchants, there is no charge to the end user. For certain micro category merchants, user may be charged a flat convenience fee which will be notified before the transaction.

Will it work on my handset?

As long as you are among 95% users using Android 4.0+ or among the iOS 7+ users, all you need is a data connection to download the app, setup your account and start OTAPing

Does it accept all cards?

Yes. No matter what card you have, OTAP will accept payments for all the merchants on-boarded on our network. It is pretty much like swiping a card, without actually needing a card, or being physically present for making the payment, or having to hand over the card and entering PIN at merchant terminal. The power of payment is in your hands, the way it should be.

What if some of my local merchants are not on the app?

Be our ninja, and share the merchant details from the option in the app. Our team will do their best in getting the merchant on-boarded ASAP
